Vendability is a cloud-based platform that allows Companies and Vendors to efficiently and effectively exchange critical risk and control information, real time and/or on a scheduled basis , integral to their business relationship and services provided.

Benefits

Vendability is designed to provide a structured risk assessment platform for Customers and Vendors using industry standard approaches. The community of members, whether customers or vendors, can learn, use and re-use industry accepted assessment questionnaires and responses, thus, facilitating in the accomplishment and approval of assessments and risk ratings.

Features

Templates are based on industry accepted standards with pre-defined questionnaires collated from the best practices of reputable companies

Replication and reuse of common responses and submittals enables faster and well-defined interaction between companies and vendors

Systematic process of assessment and remediation keeps the process on track with automated alerts.

Dashboard and analytics provides drill-down capabilities that deliver in-depth and real-time data

Cetralized cloud-hosted system system allows access from different devices and different locations.

Pricing

Vendability subscription is based on the number of users that are enrolled or registered to the system to allow smaller enterprises to afford using the system while making it cost effective for large companies.
